Choose Your Corner

Use top, bottom, tl, tr, bl, br to identify which corner to style

Top Bevel

$(this).corner("bevel top");

Top-Left Bite

$(this).corner("bite tl");

Round Bottom

$(this).corner("bottom");

Bottom-Left Notch

$(this).corner("notch bl");

Top-Right Dog Ear

$(this).corner("dog tr");

Top-Left, Bottom-Right Cool

$(this).corner("cool tl br");

Mix and Match

Chain corner calls to achieve combined effects
